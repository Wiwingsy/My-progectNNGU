Who were the first students?
Is their training different
from today’s?

We are very proud of our first graduates - after all, they set the tone and showed the whole world the level of our educational institution. Among our graduates there are people who currently hold senior positions in large companies and galleries.

Why Amsterdam? Why did
you open the first university
in this city?

The choice fell on Amsterdam not by chance.In addition to the fact that this place is imbued with the spirit of aesthetics, emancipation and waywardness, it is here that the history of one of the most famous educational institutions begins.

Why can anyone get grants?
Charity?

The possibility of providing grants is one of the main advantages of our educational institution.We welcome young outstanding talented students, even if they do not have the opportunity to pay for their studies with us on their own.
