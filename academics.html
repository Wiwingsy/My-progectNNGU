How many students have made careers after us?

The students produced by our educational institution are in great demand all over the world. The majority of students (90% according to data for 2022) from past graduates have got a high-paying job in their specialty. We follow their career path, try to help and guide them in the future.

Who says you’re the best university?
Where did you get so many awards?

The Academic Ranking of World Universities (ARWU), also known as the Shanghai Ranking, is one of the annual publications of world university rankings. It is this organization that confirms our high ranking among the world's universities. In 2019 and again in 2021, the university took 2nd place in art and design in the QS World University Rankings

Why the IT? Which is procents own students will works in IT?

For students who want to work in the field of web development, the university offers a wide range of advanced and short-term courses. The educational institution promotes an interdisciplinary approach to education and allows students to combine the study of web design with the study of graphic design, creative computer technology, communications, coding, design management and other subjects. Coding courses with specialization in HTML, CSS and Javascript, web design with specialization in HTML and CSS, User Experience (UX) Design and smartphone application design are also very popular among students.
